Decision Framework

For maximum dopamine:

  • Neon Glow (03) - Highest energy, most memorable, stands out in any README
  • Electric Wide (11) - Confident, bold, professional yet exciting
  • Terminal (06) - Authentic to CLI-first brand, developer-native
  • Shimmer (19) - Subtle animation, premium feel, distinctive

Recommendation: Start with Neon Glow as the default, offer Terminal as an alternative for developers who prefer understated aesthetics.
